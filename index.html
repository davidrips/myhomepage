FlatFinder
FlatFinder is an app I am currently building with Meteor and React Native. It helps future roommates find the house of their (mutual) dreams. After averaging out everyone's preferences, FlatFinder gives all parties the same feed of listings. Everyone swipes to like or dislike each listing, and the listings that have been liked by everyone are made easily accessible. From there, in-app features such as chat and calendar synchronization helps the future roommates coordinate themselves to see the place and sign the lease.

Beta version coming soon!

Locale
Locale is an app that allows users to earn 'locale points' by using and sharing local businesses. A user can use the app to find any food or service they are looking for in their area, but the only results they get are small, local businesses. When they check in at the business, or share it on social media, they earn a point. Locale points unlock rewards and discounts, and are used on the Community Scoreboard to determine a ranking of how 'local' all the users in the area are. Local businesses are prioritized and get free ads, while users get rewards, have fun, and can see how 'local' they really are.

Locale Beta

Pillr
Pillr is made for both an aging population and the distracted one. Users enter their prescription information, and are reminded to take each of their medicines at the right time. Users can track prescription info, such as amount of days until they need a new refill, doctor's directions, etc. In addition, Pillr checks for any interactions between your prescriptions, and alerts you if any are found. We wanted to make something that can help patients stay healthy, and help doctors and nurses keep them healthy. Pillr makes it easy to email, print, or share the user's prescription list with their healthcare provider, providing them with all the relevant information they might need.
